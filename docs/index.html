MAYA ANGELOU

Poet, Actress, Civil Right Activist(1928-2014)

10 things you didn't know about Maya Angelou:


  • Her real name was Marguerite Annie Johnson

  • She was the first black woman to conduct a cable car in San Fransisco. As a teenager, Maya Angelou earned a scholarship to study dance and drama at the California Labor School, but she briefly dropped out when she was 16 to become a cable car conductor

  • She spoke Six languages; French, Spanish, Hebrew, Italian, and Fante(a dialect of Akan native to Ghana.

  • She was one of the first African American female members in the Directors Guild of America

  • She was only the second poet in history to recite work at a presidential inauguration

  • She was an avid chef, and wrote two cookbooks

  • She didn't speak for five years in her youth.

  • She wrote and directed several movies

  • Marthin Luther, Jr was assasinated on her birthday

  • She had her own line of Hallmark greeting cards

    • "Do the best you can until you know better. Then when you know better, do better"
